About CatGPT

Thanks for checking out CatGPT, the world's first AFI(Artificial Feline Intelligence). This project was created after a joke was told in the team chat on a Friday about just how useful an artificial intelligence based on a cat personality would be. I used ChatGPT4 to write the Javascript, CSS, and HTML, troubleshoot the code, and create the list of responses.

Why did I build this pointless page when according to LinkedIn I could be 10xing my everything with "this one prompt they don't want you know about?" I like to laugh, and I like to learn. This seemed like a good opportunity to do both.
